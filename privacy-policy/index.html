Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-07-28

We care about your privacy—especially when it comes to kids. This website is built to be a safe, ad-free space for children and their families.

  • No Personal Information is Collected

    We do not collect or store any personal information when you use the interactive tools on this site. There are no accounts, logins, tracking cookies, or ads.

  • Contact Form Submissions

    If you choose to contact us through our form, your message is sent via Formspark, a third-party service. Your email address and message are only used to respond to your inquiry and are not stored by us beyond that. You can read Formspark's privacy policy here.

  • Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

    This site is designed for children under 13 but does not collect personal information from them. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise all use of the internet and contact forms.

  • California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

    We do not sell or share any personal information, and we do not track users. California residents may contact us with privacy questions at any time using the contact form.

  • Questions?

    We welcome any questions or suggestions. Please reach out using the contact form.
